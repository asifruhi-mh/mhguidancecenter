Registration Information

How to Register

Most events require advance registration. Click the "Register" button on any event or call our office at (555) 123-4567.

Payment & Fees

Many events are free. For fee-based workshops, payment is due at registration. Scholarships available for those in need.

Accessibility

All events are held in accessible locations. Please contact us if you need special accommodations or assistive services.

Childcare

Free childcare is available for most workshops and support groups. Please indicate childcare needs when registering.
