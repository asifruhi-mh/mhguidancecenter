Volunteer Opportunities

Direct Service Volunteer

4-6 hours/week

Work directly with clients providing support, assistance, and companionship in our programs.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist clients with daily living skills
  • Provide emotional support and companionship
  • Help facilitate group activities
  • Assist with program documentation

Requirements:

  • Background check required
  • Complete volunteer training program
  • Minimum 6-month commitment
  • Strong communication skills
Apply Now

Administrative Support

3-5 hours/week

Support our administrative team with office tasks, data entry, and organizational activities.

Responsibilities:

  • Data entry and filing
  • Answer phones and greet visitors
  • Assist with mailings and communications
  • Help organize events and activities

Requirements:

  • Basic computer skills
  • Reliable and punctual
  • Professional communication
  • Minimum 3-month commitment
Apply Now

Event Support Volunteer

As needed

Help make our events successful by assisting with setup, registration, and event coordination.

Responsibilities:

  • Event setup and breakdown
  • Registration and check-in
  • Assist event attendees
  • Support event coordinators

Requirements:

  • Flexible schedule
  • Friendly and welcoming demeanor
  • Ability to work in team environment
  • Physical ability to help with setup
Apply Now

Fundraising Support

2-4 hours/week

Help secure resources for our programs through fundraising activities and donor engagement.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with grant research and writing
  • Help plan fundraising events
  • Support donor communications
  • Social media promotion

Requirements:

  • Strong writing skills
  • Detail-oriented approach
  • Creative thinking abilities
  • Basic knowledge of social media
Apply Now

Professional Services

Project-based

Share your professional expertise to support our operations and enhance our services.

Areas Needed:

  • Legal services
  • Accounting and finance
  • Marketing and communications
  • IT and technology support
  • Human resources

Requirements:

  • Professional expertise in relevant field
  • Current licensing (if applicable)
  • Commitment to pro bono service
  • Understanding of non-profit sector
Apply Now
Leadership Role

Board Member

5-10 hours/month

Provide strategic leadership and governance oversight as a member of our Board of Directors.

Responsibilities:

  • Strategic planning and decision-making
  • Financial oversight and accountability
  • Community representation and advocacy
  • Fundraising and resource development

Requirements:

  • Leadership experience
  • Commitment to organization's mission
  • 3-year term commitment
  • Attend monthly board meetings
